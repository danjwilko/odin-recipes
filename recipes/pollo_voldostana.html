Pollo Voldostana

Pollo voldastana, a delicious italian recipe.

Ingrediants

  • 2 tablespoons all pupose flour
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves- pounded thin
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 10 fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 3/4 cup dry white wine
  • 3/4 cup chicken stock
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teapsonn freshly ground white pepper
  • 6 clices thinly sliced prosciutto
  • 6 slices fontina cheese

Steps

  1. Lightly flour chicken breasts, shaking off the excess flour. In a large skillet over a low heat, melt butter/margerine. Add chicken and saute until lightly browned, approx 2 mins per side. Remvoe with slotted spatual and set aside
  2. Increase heat to medium/low. Add the mushrooms and saute until juices are renderd, approx 4 mins. Add wine and simmer until reduced by 1/4, about 3-4 mins. Increase heat to meduim high, add stock, parsley and pepper and simmer until sauce is reduced to 1 cup, about 10 mins.
  3. Reduce heat to low, top each chicken breast with a slice of prosciutto and a slice of fontina cheese. Return chicken to skillet and cook just until the cheese melts. Transfer chicken to individual plates and top each with some mushroom sauce before serving.
